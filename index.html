We protect the rights of every child, focusing on the most disadvantaged. As a locally embedded, community-based organization, we understand local challenges and run youth programs that support marginalized and vulnerable children. We provide access to quality education, healthcare, nutrition, clean water, and sanitation, while protecting children from violence, abuse, climate change, and disease. In emergencies, we offer life-saving help and hope. We also provide formal and non-formal skills training to help youth earn a living. Our core areas include education, child protection, gender equality, youth empowerment, agriculture, and health building inclusive, resilient communities where all children can grow up safe, healthy, and empowered.
To protect the rights of the most disadvantaged children by providing access to quality education, healthcare, nutrition, clean water, and safety from harm. We empower youth through skills training, gender equality, and community-based programs that build sustainable futures
A world where every child regardless of their background grows up safe, healthy, educated, and empowered to reach their full potential in inclusive and resilient communities.

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Healthy Food & Nutrition

At Urban Life Charity Foundation, we believe access to healthy, nutritious food is a basic right and a cornerstone of vibrant urban communities. Good nutrition supports children’s growth, boosts immunity, improves learning outcomes, and helps families live healthier, happier lives. We work to ensure that marginalized and vulnerable urban populations can access fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and clean water. Through community gardens, nutrition education, and food assistance programs, we promote sustainable, healthy eating habits that combat malnutrition and chronic diseases.

Clean water and sanitation

Access to clean water and proper sanitation is essential for healthy urban communities. At Urban Life Charity Foundation, we work tirelessly to provide safe drinking water and improve sanitation facilities for vulnerable populations. By promoting hygiene education and installing clean water systems, we reduce waterborne diseases, protect children’s health, and improve overall quality of life. Clean water and sanitation empower families to live with dignity and build stronger, more resilient communities. We believe everyone deserves a safe and healthy environment — because clean water is life.

Health Care

Urban Life Charity Foundation is committed to improving health care access for underserved communities, ensuring that no one is left behind when it comes to receiving essential medical care. Through free check-ups, vaccination drives, maternal health support, and mental health advocacy, we strive to create a healthier future for all. By partnering with local health professionals and organizations, we provide life-saving treatments, education on preventive care, and resources to those in need. Your support—whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading awareness—helps bring hope and healing to countless individuals. Together, we can make quality health care accessible to everyone, because a healthier community is a stronger one.

Quality Education

Education is the foundation for opportunity and growth, and Urban Life Charity Foundation is dedicated to making quality education accessible to all. We believe that every child deserves the chance to learn, thrive, and shape their future. Through scholarship programs, school supply donations, mentorship initiatives, and community learning centers, we empower students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed. By partnering with educators and advocates, we bridge gaps in learning and ensure that no child is left behind due to financial or social barriers. Your support—whether through donations, volunteering, or mentorship—helps us create a world where education unlocks endless possibilities for every child.

Formal and Non-formal Skills Training

Urban Life Charity Foundation is committed to empowering individuals through both formal and non-formal skills training, ensuring access to education and career opportunities. Formal skills training provides structured learning through vocational programs, certification courses, and specialized education in fields like technology, healthcare, and business, preparing individuals for stable employment. Non-formal skills training, on the other hand, focuses on practical and adaptable learning, including hands-on workshops, apprenticeships, entrepreneurship coaching, and life skills development. By bridging these two approaches, we equip people with the knowledge, confidence, and tools to build better futures, fostering self-reliance and community growth.

Children Rights

Urban Life Charity Foundation stands for the rights of all children, including those who are marginalized, disabled, or living in underserved communities such as ghettos. Every child deserves access to education, protection, and opportunities to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. We advocate for inclusive education, ensuring that children with disabilities receive the support they need, and that students facing poverty or social barriers are given the chance to succeed. Through scholarships, mentorship programs, and safe learning spaces, we work to break down systemic obstacles and build a future where every child is valued and empowered. Together, we can create a world where no child is left behind.
